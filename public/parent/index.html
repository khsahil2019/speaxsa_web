Link Your Child's Account

To monitor attendance records, view academic performance, and stay connected with teachers, please link your child's student account.

How it works:

1

Enter Student Details

Input your child's Speaxsa student code or email to initiate the link.

2

Child Approves

Your child receives a notification and approves the parent request from their portal.

3

Access Granted

Once approved, you can view attendance, grades, and performance in real time.
