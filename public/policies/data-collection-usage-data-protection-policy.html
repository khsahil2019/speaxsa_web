1. OBJECTIVE

This Policy explains how SPEAXA collects, processes, stores, protects, uses and manages information generated through its educational ecosystem.

The objective of this Policy is to:

Protect user privacy.

Maintain confidentiality.

Improve educational services.

Ensure responsible data governance.

Comply with applicable laws.

Maintain transparency.

6. STORAGE OF INFORMATION

SPEAXA may store information using secure cloud infrastructure or other appropriate systems.

Information may be stored:

Electronically.

In encrypted databases where feasible.

In secure backups.

On authorised servers.

Within authorised internal systems.

Access is limited to personnel who require the information to perform their responsibilities.

7. DATA SECURITY

SPEAXA implements reasonable safeguards, including:

Secure authentication.

Password protection.

Multi-level access controls.

Encrypted communications where supported.

Firewalls.

Activity monitoring.

Backup procedures.

Security reviews.

Employee confidentiality obligations.

Users are also responsible for protecting their own login credentials and devices.

8. SHARING OF INFORMATION

SPEAXA does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only where reasonably necessary with:

Payment Processors.

Video Conferencing Providers.

Cloud Infrastructure Providers.

SMS Providers.

Email Service Providers.

Technical Support Providers.

Analytics Providers.

Government Authorities where required by law.

Courts or Regulatory Authorities pursuant to legal process.

Third parties are expected to protect the information they receive in accordance with applicable contractual and legal obligations.

9. INTERNATIONAL DATA PROCESSING

Where cloud infrastructure or service providers operate outside India:

Data may be processed in other jurisdictions.

SPEAXA will take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards.

Users acknowledge that cross-border processing may occur where necessary for service delivery, subject to applicable law.

10. DATA RETENTION

SPEAXA may retain information:

During active enrolment.

During teacher engagement.

To maintain academic history.

For legal compliance.

For audit purposes.

To resolve disputes.

To enforce contractual rights.

When information is no longer required, SPEAXA will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymise it, unless retention is required by law.

11. USER RESPONSIBILITIES

Users shall:

Provide accurate information.

Keep information updated.

Protect passwords.

Report suspected misuse immediately.

Avoid sharing account credentials.

Use the Platform responsibly.

12. DATA BREACH RESPONSE

If SPEAXA becomes aware of a significant data security incident, it may:

Investigate the incident.

Take corrective measures.

Restore affected systems.

Notify affected users where required by applicable law.

Cooperate with competent authorities where legally required.

13. USER RIGHTS

Subject to applicable law, users may request to:

Access their personal information.

Correct inaccurate information.

Update account details.

Delete eligible information.

Withdraw consent where applicable.

Close their account.

Certain information may continue to be retained where necessary to comply with legal obligations or protect legitimate business interests.

14. DATA GENERATED THROUGH AI & ANALYTICS

SPEAXA may use analytics and AI-assisted tools to:

Recommend learning pathways.

Generate academic insights.

Identify learning gaps.

Improve educational outcomes.

Support teachers.

Enhance platform functionality.

AI-assisted outputs are intended to support, and not replace, professional educational judgment.

15. POLICY REVIEW

SPEAXA may update this Policy from time to time to reflect:

Changes in law.

Technology upgrades.

Security enhancements.

Platform improvements.

Operational requirements.

Updated versions become effective upon publication unless otherwise required by law.

16. CONTACT

Questions relating to this Policy may be submitted through SPEAXA's official support channels.
